Social Circuit App

Design / App / Prototyping

Social Circuit is the name of a mobile phone app I, along with a team, have prototyped using the User Centered Design paradigm.

The goal of this project was not to create a fully function app, but to gather qualitative data on an app idea and find what users wanted in it. This was not merely surveys. Our team went out and completed enthography studies amongst college students, party-goers, people whom frequent coffee shops, etc... Using careful word choice and conducting non-chalant coversation, we gathered enough info to start on a low-fidelity mock-up, did some user testing, and reiterated the process until we came up with a prototype that our focus test users enjoyed. Once that was finshed, we completed a high-fidelity prototype for presentation.

During the period of this project, our team and I became very familiar with many quantitative and qualitative research approaches and gathering key information from users since users do not always perfectly describe what they want or need.

You can view some files we have availble for display.
