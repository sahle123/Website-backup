AfterLife

Unity / Code / Game

A game I worked on for one of my senior projects. The goals of this project was to complete a game a that fulfilled 2 of the 8 aesthetics described in the MDA framework. These aesthetics I have completed are Challenge and Exploration.

AfterLife is a short first person platformer where the goal is to collect enough souls to win the game. I have taken many inspirations from The Legend of Zelda series and Dark Souls. The game is not very long and can be beaten in about 10 - 15 mins by a very competent player.

Enjoy.

Technologies Used
Unity3D, C#, JavaScript, Gimp, Pen and Paper sketches
